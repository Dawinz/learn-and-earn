## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our App:

#### Account Information
- **User Authentication Data:** Username, email address, and encrypted password
- **Profile Information:** Display name, profile picture (if provided), user preferences

#### Learning Activity Data
- **Progress Information:** Lessons completed, quizzes taken, quiz scores and results

- **Streaks and Statistics:** Daily login streaks, learning time, completion rates

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

#### Device Information
- **Device Identifiers:** Device model, operating system version, Android version
- **App Information:** App version, build number, installation ID
- **Device Settings:** Language preferences, timezone, screen resolution
- **Hardware Information:** Available storage space, memory usage

#### Usage Data
- **App Usage Statistics:** Features accessed, time spent in app, navigation patterns
- **Session Information:** Login times, logout times, session duration
- **Interaction Data:** Buttons clicked, screens viewed, actions performed
- **Performance Metrics:** App crashes, errors, loading times, response times

#### Network and Connectivity Information
- **Network Type:** WiFi, mobile data, connection quality
- **IP Address:** For security and fraud prevention purposes
- **Connectivity Status:** Online/offline status

### 1.3 Local Storage Data

The following information is stored locally on your device:
- **Cached Content:** Lessons, quiz questions, learning materials
- **Preferences:** App settings, notification preferences, theme choices
- **Temporary Data:** Session tokens, authentication credentials
- **Progress Sync Data:** Pending progress updates awaiting synchronization

#### Google AdMob
Our App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:
- **Advertising ID:** Your device's advertising identifier
- **Ad Interaction Data:** Ads viewed, ads clicked, ad engagement metrics
- **Device Information:** For ad targeting and personalization

For more information, please review:
- [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 4. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement various measures to protect it:

### 4.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols
- **Secure Storage:** User passwords are hashed using strong cryptographic algorithms
- **Access Controls:** Strict access controls limit who can access user data
- **Regular Audits:** We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

### 4.2 Organizational Safeguards
- **Employee Training:** Our team is trained on privacy and security best practices
- **Limited Access:** Only authorized personnel have access to personal information
- **Confidentiality Agreements:** All team members sign confidentiality agreements
- **Incident Response:** We have procedures in place to respond to security incidents

### 4.3 Your Responsibilities
- **Strong Passwords:** Use strong, unique passwords for your account
- **Device Security:** Keep your device secure with a PIN, password, or biometric lock
- **Account Monitoring:** Regularly review your account activity for suspicious behavior
- **Reporting:** Immediately report any suspected unauthorized access to your account

### 4.4 Limitations

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to using commercially reasonable means to protect your personal information.

## 19. Glossary of Terms

**Personal Information:** Information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with a particular user.

**Device Information:** Technical information about the device used to access the App.

**Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with the App.

**Cookies:** Small data files stored on your device (primarily used by web services; limited use in mobile apps).

**Advertising ID:** A unique, user-resettable identifier for advertising purposes provided by mobile operating systems.

**Encryption:** The process of converting information into a secure format that is unreadable without the proper decryption key.

**Anonymization:** The process of removing personally identifiable information so that individuals can no longer be identified.

**Data Controller:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data (Learn & Grow).

**Data Processor:** An entity that processes data on behalf of the data controller (our service providers).
