# Assets and Screenshots\n\n## Required assets\n- **App icon**: 512x512 px, PNG, max 1MB\n- **Feature graphic**: 1024x500 px, PNG/JPG, max 1MB\n- **Screenshots** (Phone): at least 2, up to 8; 1080x1920+ (portrait) or 1920x1080+ (landscape)\n- **Tablet 7-inch** (optional): at least 1\n- **Tablet 10-inch** (optional): at least 1\n- **Promo video** (optional): YouTube URL\n\n## Filename conventions\nUse lowercase, hyphenated names, e.g., `feature-graphic.png`, `screenshot-01.png`.\n\n## Screenshot guidance\n- Capture primary user journey: onboarding, dashboard, lessons, rewards redemption\n- Avoid sensitive personal data in screenshots\n- Ensure consistent theme (avoid white backgrounds per brand preference)\n- Localize screenshots if publishing in multiple languages\n\n## How to export from emulator/device\n- Use Android Studio Emulator (Pixel 6) or a physical device\n- Use: adb exec-out screencap -p > screenshot.png (or built-in capture)\n\n## Where to place raw files in repo\nPlace working files under `play-store/assets/`:\n- `play-store/assets/icon/`\n- `play-store/assets/feature-graphic/`\n- `play-store/assets/screenshots/`\n
